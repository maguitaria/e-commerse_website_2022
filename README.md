# 🛒 E-Commerce Wonderland 🌟

## 🚀 Blast Off to Shopping Bliss!

Welcome to our dazzling e-commerce website, a fantastic creation born out of the HTML and CSS programming course! 🎉
This is my first project in my coding journey, that`s why it is so important for me. Pay attention to all our features and... yes, this was made only after 4 months of starting learning coding world!

[E-Commerce Wonderland](https://maguitaria.github.io/e-commerse_website_2022/)

## 🌈 Features that Sparkle

### Responsive Design 📱

- Our website boasts 10 pages that dance gracefully on screens of all sizes - from the grandest desktops to the tiniest mobile phones!

### Magic Currency Change 💸

- Switch between currencies with a wave of your virtual wand! Shopping is truly enchanting when you can pay in your preferred currency.

### Shopping Cart Bonanza 🛒

- Our magical cart holds your chosen treasures and lets you manage them with ease. It's like having your very own shopping assistant!

### Friendly Contact Elves 🧝‍♂️

- Connect with us effortlessly using our friendly contact form. We're here to answer your questions and receive your feedback.

### Figma Design Enchantment ✨

- Our bewitching UI design was conjured up using Figma, ensuring an eye-catching and user-friendly experience.

### Spellbinding Animations 🪄

- Be mesmerized by the captivating animations that bring our website to life. It's not just shopping; it's an experience!

## ✨ Technologies that Power the Magic

- **Frontend Spells:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
- **Backend Wizardry:** PHP

## 🌟 How to Join the Adventure

1. Clone this repository and let the magic begin!
2. Open the `index.html` file in your favorite browser to embark on a shopping adventure.
3. Use the currency change spell, shopping cart magic, and contact form potions to interact with the website.
